Adam & Eve

Adam and Eve was filmed using a Canon DSLR T6i on a tripod. The final project was edited using Adobe Premiere Pro 2019. The short film was filmed on Sunday, October 21, 2018 from 4 PM to 6 PM at Monique Carchi's backyard and bedroom. During pre-production, a storyboard of 12 panels was created as the first official draft for production. Later, a video board was created, followed by a shot list. Lastly, a mock script was created and shortly after, production began. Some of the difficulties during production included:
    -Background noise
    -Microphone issues
    -Cold weather (due to required costumes, actors were not dressed appropriately for weather)
    -Actor did not have complete costume (no white pants, all shots had to be filmed waist up)
    -Condensing footage
    -Loss of natural sunlight
    -No cinematographer (actors had to film themselves)
Postproduction included the video editing process, which consisted of cutting, sounds, sequencing, and voice over.
